Architecture summary

How the PoC is split

The dual-rail model is easier to understand when the three disclosure surfaces are shown separately.

  • Settlement rail Liquid Testnet provides confidential on-chain settlement evidence.
  • Travel Rule rail IVMS101 is persisted off-chain and linked by settlement reference and txid.
  • Audit rail The demo blinding key stands in for secure case-management disclosure material.
